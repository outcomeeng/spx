# Outcome Engineering

PROVIDES Outcome Engineering methodology primitives for change coordination, durable product truth, agent workflow continuity, and verification workflow integration
SO THAT spec-tree libraries, change domains, agent harnesses, interfaces, and surfaces
CAN share one product model for work intake, refinement, execution, and evidence

### Compliance

- ALWAYS: Outcome Engineering primitives describe backend-neutral product concepts before any CLI, repository-local, hosted, or agent-specific surface exposes them ([audit])
- ALWAYS: Outcome Engineering primitives distinguish domain semantics, interface contracts, and surface interaction boundaries before a consumer adapts them ([audit])
